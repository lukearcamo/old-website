I'm an ordinary -year-old student currently living in Canada. I decided to create this website to record and showcase the things I've made throughout my life. My interests lie in art and coding, and my favourite projects are the ones that allow me to combine both.

Artist

I have been doing traditional art since I was very young. But it was only during the pandemic that I discovered digital art and began taking art seriously. Over the course of a year, my art evolved through many different styles and subjects, from landscapes to portraits, but most of them have one common theme: they are accompanied by stories.

I would describe my preferred style as semi-realistic and painterly, though my tastes also include realistic, surreal, and minimalistic art, as well as traditional art from various cultures. I also love digital paintings & renders, and many digital artists who I've found through Instagram, YouTube, and Reddit have influenced my own art and taught me many skills.

Some artists whose artwork have inspired me include Johannes Vermeer, Vincent Van Gogh, The Group of Seven & Emily Carr, Claude Monet, and Edward Hopper.

I often describe myself as a dreamer. But what does that really mean?

I have always loved creating new things. I enjoy escaping from reality, entering into the depths of my own mind, and re-emerging to the outside world with ideas from my wild imagination. This is why, aside from art and coding, I also love reading, music, worldbuilding (as you may have seen from the descriptions on my artwork page), video games, and conlanging.

But these media are not limited to being output only, because in order to inspire and feed the imagination, input is required as well. I love learning all sorts of things, especially knowledge that profoundly changes my outlook on life. The small ideas I learn each day are what lead me down deep and bifurcating research rabbit holes, offering more and more ideas with which I feed my next creation.
