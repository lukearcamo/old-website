Artist

I have been doing traditional art since I was very young. But it was only during the pandemic that I discovered digital art and began taking art seriously. Over the course of a year, my art evolved through many different styles and themes. Now, I am able to paint a variety of subjects, from landscapes to portraits, most of them accompanied by rich stories. I would describe my style as semi-realistic and sometimes painterly, though my tastes also sometimes include realistic and minimalistic art.

I often describe myself as a dreamer. But what does that actually mean?

I have always loved creating new things. I enjoy escaping from reality, entering into the depths of my own mind, and re-emerging to the outside world with ideas from my wild imagination. This is why, aside from art and coding, I also love reading, music, worldbuilding (as you may have seen from the descriptions on my artwork page), video games, and linguistics.

But in order to inspire me and feed my imagination, input is required as well. I love learning all sorts of things, especially knowledge that profoundly changes my outlook on life. Sometimes, one small idea leads me down a fascinating internet rabbit hole that inspires my next creation. Other times, it is my creation that inspires research.
